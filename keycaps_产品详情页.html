Choose the Phoenix Series by Unico and join us on a journey of continuous innovation and long-term success in the arcade industry.

The Phoenix Series from Unico offers a dedicated display solution for traditional arcade cabinets.


Traditionally, most arcade machines relied on CRT monitors ranging from 17"" to 30"".

However, as decades have passed, many of these CRT displays have deteriorated or become hard to source.


The Phoenix Monitor Series product was thus created.


 The Phoenix Series—featuring models such as ULM17, ULM19, ULM26,

and the upcoming ULM30—provides a reliable, high-quality alternative to keep your classic cabinets running.
