About UQ71 Series

Inspired by "maglev" and "flight", the UQ71 combines futuristic design with elite performance. Its 71-key compact layout preserves a full-function zone, striking a perfect balance between efficiency and portability. Suspended keycaps float above the body, complemented by streamlined dynamic light strips that instantly ignite a competitive atmosphere.


At its core is our self-developed magnetic levitation switch, featuring an ultra-low actuation distance of just 0.05mm and an ultra-high 8000Hz polling rate. With per-user customization, you can respond to the game at lightning speed. Every key offers adjustable actuation points and sensitivity, supporting personalized key remapping and macro recording — so each press matches your unique rhythm.


With full anti-ghosting and N-key rollover, you can execute every combo flawlessly. Dynamic RGB lighting effects and convenient sound mode switching help you craft your team’s signature style. A sturdy aluminum alloy top plate and shock-absorbing base structure deliver long-lasting tactile stability.


The UQ71 is not just a high-performance keyboard — it's your winning edge, combining cutting-edge engineering and esports aesthetics for the ultimate competitive advantage.
