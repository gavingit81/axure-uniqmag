At Uniqmag, we are focused on transforming the way you interact with your computer. Our vision is centered around designing a keyboard that combines advanced technology with sophisticated engineering without unnecessary flair. We’re here to fundamentally upgrade the core performance and functionality of keyboards for gamers and professionals alike.



Our leadership team brings deep technical expertise and extensive industry experience. With a solid background in AI and tech innovation, our co-founder Nancy Zhou set a clear strategic direction, while Horson Li—armed with over a decade of experience in magnetic technologies from his work on magnetic accessories and motors—has driven the breakthrough innovations at the heart of our products. Their combined efforts are further strengthened by William Ma’s expertise in consumer electronics and global brand strategy in the U.S. and Europe.



The cornerstone of our technology is the introduction of magnetic floating switches based on advanced magnetic induction chips. By eliminating traditional physical springs, our design achieves a smoother, more consistent actuation. The result is a highly responsive keyboard that supports an impressive 8K polling rate, ensuring every input is registered with precision—even under the high demands of competitive gaming.


But we didn’t stop there. Recognizing that modern users require more than just speed and precision, we have integrated AI-driven features into our design. Our keyboard is engineered to learn user preferences and evolve with usage patterns, effectively working as a smart control hub that manages your peripherals and streamlines your gaming or professional setup.


We are also pioneering a unique 3-mode magnetic axis configuration that offers dynamic performance adjustments. This innovative approach not only allows for superior tactile feedback but also sets a new standard in customization—enabling users to fine-tune key responses to exactly suit their personal needs.


Uniqmag is the result of relentless dedication and a willingness to challenge the status quo. While many in the keyboard market continue to focus solely on external design, we pledge to push the boundaries of what a keyboard can truly deliver. As we prepare to launch our first groundbreaking product, our goal is clear: to equip every enthusiast, gamer, and professional with a keyboard that offers exceptional performance, unparalleled customization, and forward-thinking smart features—reflecting a new era in input technology.
